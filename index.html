About

Aishu lab is high-tech company focusing on migration, operations, maintenance and software development on public and hybrid cloud platforms.

Aishu aims to be a world-leading software and service provider in cloud computing. Aishu's business is all about the Cloud. Aishu lab headquarter is located in Bengaluru and also planning to expand its offices in abroad. Aishu provides professional technical consulting, training and software development services for multinational companies and startups in space of analytics powered by AI , Devops & infrastructure and platform and app development.

Aishu is a Development Partner of Leading Cloud Provider - Microsoft and AWS.
